START COOKING

  • fry the bacon up in a pan. When it's halfway cooked, add a little bit of maple syrup. Stir it up so that it coats all the bacon
  • toss some butter in a frying pan and heat it up and put two big shredded potato patties (make a ball with them and then flatten them out on the pan) and fry it up
  • turn the heat on halfway for the eggs. THIS TAKES SOME CONCENTRATION: whisk the eggs so they're all scrambled and let them cook. Don't let them sit for more than 30 seconds without whisking them up. You don't want to burn them at the bottom
  • Sometime around the halfway mark with the eggs, turn on the toaster. Go right back to those eggs and make sure you don't screw them up.
  • once everything is nice and cooked, plate away
